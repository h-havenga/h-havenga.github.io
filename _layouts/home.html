Welcome dear reader, as you may have noticed I'm Henno. On the right you can see me running in the beautiful woods of Finland. Apart from running, what could considered an unhealthy amount, I study the atmosphere and the interesting processes in it. I'm particularly fascinated by severe weather and the impact it has on socio-economics. My studies have allowed me access to some cool technological stuff, this has led me to become an avid GNU\Linux user and I've started learning some programming languages, while not an expert by any means, I do have an inquisitive nature and maybe a little OCD, which helps. My interests spread beyond these things obviously. I don't question your motives for being here. If you're a spy agency feel free to contact me personally, if you just Googled me you can find some topics here to bring up during a conversation and just overall I hope you like what you see.

This blog was created in the hope to my share my data, code, knowledge (or lack thereof) and get critiqued in the process. I will post some personal viewpoints here, don't take me too seriously. I'm a nice guy in real life, I like beer and puppies. Be aware that you are only getting a glimpse into snippets my life, I would not recommend drawing too many conclusions about me here.
